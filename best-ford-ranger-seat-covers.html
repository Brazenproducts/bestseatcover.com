Best Ford Ranger Seat Covers of 2026

Top picks for 2019-2024 Ranger owners who need protection without ruining the truck's clean midsize cabin

The return of the Ford Ranger gave midsize truck buyers something they had been missing: a truck that feels smaller and easier to live with than an F-150, but still looks and works like a real pickup. That also means Ranger owners usually want seat covers that preserve that cleaner, tighter cabin feel rather than turning it into a bulky aftermarket mess.

For the 2019-2024 Ranger, fitment matters. The seat shapes are not huge like a full-size truck, and loose covers stand out fast. We ranked the options that make the most sense for Ranger owners who use their truck for commuting, weekend trips, dogs, gear, and occasional dirty work without wanting to overpay.

~$140-$190

Coverado takes the top spot for the Ranger because it offers the cleanest mix of fit, appearance, and practicality for a midsize truck interior.
