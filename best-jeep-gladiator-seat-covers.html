Best Jeep Gladiator Seat Covers of 2026

Protection that matches how you actually use your truck — ranked for 2020-2026 Gladiator models

The Gladiator occupies a unique spot: it's a Jeep with a truck bed, which means owners tend to use it harder than a standard pickup. Seat covers for the Gladiator need to handle trail grime, work gear, and the general chaos of owning a vehicle you're not afraid to get dirty. The good news is that several brands now offer custom-fit options specifically patterned for JT seats.

Getting the right cover for the Gladiator means paying attention to SRS airbag compatibility (side airbags deploy through the seat), proper console pass-through design, and materials that can survive UV exposure when the top comes off. Budget universals might look fine on day one, but they rarely survive a real Jeep lifestyle.
