Best Seat Covers for Dogs of 2026

Our favorite back-seat protectors for muddy paws, shedding, and daily dog hauling

#1 4Knines Dog Rear Seat Cover... #2 Bartact Custom Seat Covers #3 Active Pets Dog Back Seat C... #4 Meadowlark Dog Seat Cover

Dog seat covers do a different job than vehicle-specific seat covers. They need to resist claws, trap hair, block drool and mud, and stay in place while a moving dog does what moving dogs do. The best ones are the products that manage to protect the seat without turning the whole back row into a noisy, sliding tarp.

This list mixes dedicated dog hammock covers with one important exception: Bartact. If you own a Jeep or supported truck and your dog rides up front or regularly trashes the factory upholstery, a true heavy-duty custom seat cover can be the smarter long-term solution than a removable pet blanket.

The Bottom Line

For most people, 4Knines is the best dedicated dog seat cover. If you drive a supported Jeep or truck and your dog is hard on interiors, Bartact may actually be the smarter long-term move. Active Pets is the best value hammock cover, and Meadowlark is a good feature-rich alternative for organized pet travel.
